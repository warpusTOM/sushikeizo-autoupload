Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 2, 2026

1. Overview

Sushikeizo AutoUpload is a desktop creator workflow for preparing Roblox gameplay clips, captions, and posting metadata for short-form video platforms.

2. Information We Use

When you connect TikTok, the app may use information authorized by you through TikTok Login Kit, such as your basic profile information, public video list, upload permissions, and publishing permissions. The app may also use local video files, captions, hashtags, and posting settings that you provide.

3. How Information Is Used

Information is used to connect your TikTok account, prepare video uploads, publish or upload videos when you approve that action, review video performance, and improve your content workflow.

4. Storage

TikTok access tokens and refresh tokens may be stored locally on your device so the workflow can stay connected. Local video files and generated captions remain on your device unless you choose to upload or publish them.

5. Sharing

Information is shared with TikTok only as needed to use TikTok API features you authorize. The app does not sell personal information.

6. Your Choices

You can disconnect the app by revoking access in your TikTok account settings. You can also delete local files and tokens from your device.

7. Contact

For privacy questions, contact the app owner through the official creator website or channel where this policy is published.
